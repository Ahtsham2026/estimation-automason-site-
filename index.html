What you actually get

Not a generic tool. Yours.

Every estimation company tags differently, sorts differently, and outputs to a different template. Generic software forces you to adapt to it. We build the tool around how you already work.

01

Configured to your tags

Your tag prefixes, your aliases, your shortcuts. Whether your team writes "B:" or "BRK" or "breaker_1p" — the tool knows it's the same thing and treats it accordingly.

02

Mapped to your sections

Circuit breakers, panels, distribution, fire alarm, low voltage — items land in the right section automatically, in the order your senior estimator would put them.

03

Output in your format

The cleaned file comes back in your house template — your columns, your formatting, your headers. Ready for pricing the moment it lands. No reformatting needed.

04

Runs in your browser

Your tool, hosted on your own URL. Estimators upload exports, the tool processes, they download the cleaned file. No software to install, no IT involvement.

Common questions

What people ask before they send a sample.

Q1

Will it work with my company's tag format?

Yes — that's the entire point. The tool is configured to your specific tag conventions during setup. Whether your team writes "B: 1P/20A Breaker" or "BRK-1P20" or some shorthand only your estimators understand, we map it correctly so the cleaned output looks exactly like what your senior estimator would produce.

Q2

What takeoff software do you support?

PlanSwift exports today, where most of our work is concentrated. If you're on Bluebeam, McCormick, Accubid, On-Screen Takeoff, or another system, reach out — we're expanding based on demand and can let you know our timeline.

Q3

How do I know it won't miss items or break on edge cases?

Before handover, you compare the cleaned output to a manual cleanup of the same file. If it doesn't match what your senior estimator would produce, we fix it. You only pay setup once it's working on your real workflow — not before.

Q4

How long does setup take?

Roughly a week from your first sample to a configured, tested tool. We need to understand your tag conventions, your section structure, and your output format. Then we configure, test against your files, and hand it over with credentials.

Q5

Do I need to schedule a call?

No. The whole point is removing meetings from the equation. Send one sample export, we'll handle the rest async. We only get on a call if you specifically want to — most clients never do.

Q6

What does the monthly fee cover?

Hosting your tool, ongoing maintenance, updates as your tag conventions evolve, and direct support whenever something needs adjustment. Month-to-month, no contract, cancel anytime.
